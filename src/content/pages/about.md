I'm a proud family patriarch and occasional developer of ill repute. At work I'm co-founder and CEO at [Speedscale](https://speedscale.com), where we help companies test applications so they run faster with fewer errors in production.

Outside of work I've been an open source person for a long time. My dad got me onto Ubuntu in 2007 (he was putting Linux on the lowest-powered box in the house to use as a firewall), and I've run Linux at home ever since. These days that's a k3s homelab on a Raspberry Pi and a growing pile of AI models running locally on hardware I already own. I worked at Pentaho back in 2014 when everything was open source, and that mindset stuck.
